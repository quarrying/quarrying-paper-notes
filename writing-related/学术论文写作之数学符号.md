    <  lt less than
    <= le less than or equal to
    >  gt greater than
    >= ge greater than or equal to
    == eq equal to
    != ne not equal to

$\hat x$, 读作 x hat

$\tilde x$, 读作 x tilde

$x'$, 读作 x prime

$x''$, 读作 x double prime

$x'''$, 读作 x triple prime
